MEDPLUS

  • Follow the Prescription

    Always take the medicine exactly as prescribed by your healthcare provider. Do not alter the dosage or schedule without consulting them..

  • Read the Label

    Carefully read the medication label and any accompanying information to understand how to take it correctly.

  • Know the Active Ingredients

    Be aware of the active ingredients to avoid accidental overdose, especially if taking multiple medications.

  • Check for Allergies

    Ensure you are not allergic to any ingredients in the medication. If you are unsure, consult your healthcare provider.

  • Avoid Interactions

    Be mindful of potential interactions with other medications, supplements, or foods. Inform your healthcare provider about all other substances you are taking.

  • Take with Water

    Most medications should be taken with a full glass of water unless instructed otherwise.

  • Timing Matters

    Follow specific timing instructions, such as taking the medication with food, on an empty stomach, or at a particular time of day.

  • Storage Instructions

    Store medications as directed, usually in a cool, dry place. Some may require refrigeration.

  • Do Not Share Medication

    Never share your medication with others, even if they have similar symptoms.

  • Monitor for Side Effects

    Be aware of common side effects and seek medical advice if you experience any unusual or severe reactions.

  • Avoid Alcohol

    Many medications interact negatively with alcohol, so it is generally best to avoid it unless otherwise advised by your healthcare provider.
